Electronics

The electronics fundamentals course teaches the basics of electronic devices, circuits, and systems. Students learn about Ohm's Law, circuit analysis techniques, and electronic components such as resistors, capacitors, and transistors.

Physics

A physics course teaches the fundamental principles governing the natural world, from classical mechanics to quantum physics. Students learn to solve problems and analyze physical phenomena using mathematical models and experimental data.

Web Develoment

Web development is the work involved in developing a website for the Internet (World Wide Web) or an intranet (a private network).
